About Enchanté Cosmetics

Enchanté Cosmetics was founded in 2024 by Jessica Mahlangu, a passionate makeup artist with years of experience in the industry. Frustrated by the lack of makeup options that catered to diverse skin tones without compromising on quality, Jessica set out to create a brand that would fill this gap. We are dedicated to providing high-end professional makeup products formulated for everyday use. Our makeup not only enhances your natural beauty but also offers necessary protective qualities, ensuring your skin stays healthy and radiant. With a focus on inclusivity, we offer a diverse range of 24 colours designed to complement both ethnic and Caucasian skin tones. From fair to deep, we have shades that cater to everyone, allowing you to express yourself with confidence. Driven by a commitment to excellence and innovation, our business strives to empower individuals of all backgrounds to feel beautiful and confident in their own skin. Our mission is to revolutionize the makeup industry by providing premium products that meet the needs of every customer, regardless of skin type or tone.
